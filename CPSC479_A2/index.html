Color Compositions Gallery

A display of the original images, overlay of the images with their corresponding color filters, and then their appropriate alignment.

My Alignment Algorithm:
I split alignment into three steps: 1. First, calculate the edge maps of each colored filter. 2. Using one of the edge maps (blue color) as a base, transform the other two channel edge maps in many small different iterations. 3. Calculate the distances of corresponding edge maps, and select the two edge maps per channel with smallest deviation from the base. 4. Overlay the transformed color channels over each other, and save as image.
